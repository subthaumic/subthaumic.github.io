The EP Mathematics and Data organizes a [lecture series](https://wiki.structures.mathi.uni-heidelberg.de/index.php?title=Activities#Lecture_Series:_Mathematics_and_Data) featuring talks on foundational ideas, as well as exciting novelties in the field of topological and geometrical data analysis.  

Furthermore, there are a number of lectures, seminars and workshops - at Heidelberg University and beyond - that are loosely related to the EP Mathematics and Data.
