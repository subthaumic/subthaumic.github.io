Topological Data Analysis (TDA) offers a nonparametric toolkit for uncovering structure in complex data. The Winter 2019/20 journal club combined foundational readings with applications drawn from machine learning, cosmology, neuroscience, and multivariate statistics.

## Coordinates
- **Time:** Wednesdays, 09:15 – 10:45  
- **Location:** Mathematikon, Seminar Room 9  
- **Format:** Weekly presentations based on the listed papers, with additional references allowed.  
- **Organisers:** Michael Bleher & Daniel Spitz — [structures-hiwi@mathi.uni-heidelberg.de](mailto:structures-hiwi@mathi.uni-heidelberg.de)  
- **Mailing list:** Registration via [Müsli](https://muesli.mathi.uni-heidelberg.de)
