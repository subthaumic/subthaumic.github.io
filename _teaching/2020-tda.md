The Summer 2020 journal club continued the exploration of topological data analysis (TDA) with an emphasis on virtual meetings and hands-on computations. Sessions alternated between review talks on core methods and discussions of recent applications.

## Coordinates
- **Time:** Tuesdays (even weeks 16:15 – 17:45, odd weeks ~17:00 – 18:00)  
- **Location:** Zoom (ID 830 4593 4507; password on request)  
- **Organisers:** Michael Bleher, Maximilian Schmahl & Daniel Spitz — [structures-hiwi@mathi.uni-heidelberg.de](mailto:structures-hiwi@mathi.uni-heidelberg.de)  
- **Materials:** Recordings and notes hosted on [MaMpf](https://mampf.mathi.uni-heidelberg.de/)
